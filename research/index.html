My research interests are in mathematical physics, supersymmetric gauge theories and string theory.

Publications

  • AB and Jan Troost, Counting the Massive Vacua of N=1* Super Yang-Mills Theory

    Published in JHEP 1603 (2016) 163 (arxiv)
  • AB and Jan Troost, On the N=1* Gauge Theory on a Circle and Ellipic Integrable Systems

    Published in JHEP 07 (2016) 036 (arxiv)

Strings, Cosmology and Gravity Student Conference

I am one of the organizers of this international conference that will take place on 7-8 january, 2016 at Imperial College, London.
The conference is designed to bring together young PhD and postdoctoral scientists working in theoretical high energy physics, especially in the areas of String Theory, Cosmology and Gravity.

More information can be found on the conference website, and on the poster.
